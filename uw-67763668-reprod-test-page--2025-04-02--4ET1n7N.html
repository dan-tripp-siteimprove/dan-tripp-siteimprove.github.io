Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)

Why should I study here?

  • Ranked as the seventh top university in Asia.
  • Ranked #28 for Computer Science and #29 for Mathematics according to 2018 QS World Rankings.
  • The campus, located in Shatin, covers 137.3 hectares and is described as the most scenic and sustainably designed and maintained campus in Hong Kong, complete with seven libraries, a swimming pool, 12 tennis courts, five indoor gyms and an archery practice range.
  • Has exchange partnerships with 280 schools in 35 countries and welcomes 3,400 visiting students a year.

City University of Hong Kong (CityU)

Why should I study here?

  • Ranked as the fifteenth top university in Asia.
  • Ranked as the fourth-best school in the 2020 QS Top 50 under 50 Rankings.
  • Ranked in the top 51-100 for mathematics and computer science according to the 2019 QS Rankings.
  • The campus, located in Kowloon, covers 15.6 hectares and is conveniently situated near local shopping malls and public transit. Hu Fa Kuang Sports Centre is a five-storey sports centre which houses a multi-purpose hall and four practice gymnasiums for badminton, basketball, volleyball, martial arts and dance.
  • CityU has exchange links with over 450 partner universities and welcomes over 1000 exchange students annually.
