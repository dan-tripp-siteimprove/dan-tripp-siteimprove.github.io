Welcome to Highland University

At Highland University, we are committed to providing exceptional education that prepares students for successful careers and meaningful lives. Our diverse range of programs serves students at every stage of their educational journey.

Undergraduate Programs

Our undergraduate programs offer a comprehensive liberal arts education combined with specialized training in your chosen field. Students can select from over 80 majors across our colleges of Arts and Sciences, Business, Engineering, and Health Sciences.

Highland University's undergraduate experience goes beyond the classroom. Our students engage in hands-on research, internships with leading companies, and study abroad opportunities in more than 30 countries.

Graduate Programs

Highland's graduate programs are designed for working professionals and full-time students seeking advanced degrees. We offer Master's programs in fields ranging from Education and Psychology to Computer Science and Public Policy.

Our faculty members are leaders in their fields, bringing cutting-edge research and real-world expertise to every course. Small class sizes ensure personalized attention and mentorship throughout your graduate journey.

MBA Programs

The Highland MBA is ranked among the top programs in the region. Whether you choose our traditional full-time MBA, part-time evening MBA, or Executive MBA, you'll gain the skills and network needed to advance your career.

Our MBA curriculum emphasizes leadership, innovation, and global business perspectives. Students work on consulting projects with real companies and have access to our extensive alumni network across diverse industries.

Online Learning

Highland University offers a growing selection of fully online degree programs and certificates. Our online programs maintain the same rigorous academic standards as our on-campus offerings while providing the flexibility you need to balance work, family, and education.

Online students have access to dedicated support services, virtual library resources, and interactive learning platforms. You'll engage with faculty and classmates through live video sessions, discussion forums, and collaborative projects.
